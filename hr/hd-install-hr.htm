Priprema za HD instalaciju

Za normalan korištaj preporučujemo ext4 file-sistem; to je glavni file-sistem za aptosid i dobro podržavan.

Prije instalacije molimo vas, izvucite sve usb-stickove, kamere, itd.. Instaliranje na USB uređaje treba više pripravka. Možete editirati instaler datoteku: ~/.sidconf, i tako koristiti druge file-sisteme ili instaliranje na više particija, na primjer separatna /home particija

Jako preporučujemo da imate posebnu particiju za podatke. U slučaju nesreće, stabilnost i sigurnost vaših podataka ne možete platiti s novcem.

Zato je vaš $HOME mjesto gdje osnovne konfiguracije aplikacija su memorirane. Ili da kažemo da drugi način, direktorij za aplikacije da upišu postavke.

Re-instaliranje aplikacija da instalirate ili duplicirate drugo računalo

Da napravite listu vaših instaliranih programa, da duplicirate instaliranu bazu na drugo računalo, ili možda ako morate reinstalirati zbog bilo kojeg razloga, u konzoli

dpkg -l|awk '/^ii/{ print $2 }'|grep -v -e ^lib -e -dev -e $(uname -r) >/home/username/installed.txt

Onda kopirajte text datoteku na usb stick ili drugi medij koja ja vama pri ruci.

Na novom računalu kopirajte datoteku u $HOME i koristite listu kao referenciju da instalirate vaše potrebne aplikacije.

RAM i Swap

Na računalima s manje nego 512 MB RAM morate imati swap-particiju. Veličina ne bi trebala biti manja nego 128 mb (cfdisk-output-u ne smijete vjerovati, zato sto računa sa bazom 10), više nego 1 GB swap je uvijek u redu.

Pogledajte molimo vas ovdje: Particijoniranje vašeg hard-diska

UVIJEK OSIGURAJTE SVOJE PODATKE! uključujući favorite i emajle! See Back-Up with rdiff and Back-Up with rsync. Another option is sbackup (needs installing).

Instalacija na hard-disk je puno udobnija i puno brža, nego da radite s Live-CD.

Kao prvo, morate postaviti redoslijed za start u BIOS na CD-ROM. Na većinom računala možete uči u BIOS-setup s pritiskom na [del] pri startu (s nekim BIOS-verzijama možete izabrati boot uređaj pri startu, s AMI-BIOS, s F11 ili F8).

aptosid bih trebao startovati u većinom slučaja. Ako ne, možete koristiti bootopcije, (zvani cheatcodes) koje možete upisati u naredbenu crtu boot menadžera. Korištaj boot parametara (na primjer za monitor rezoluciju ili jezik selekciju možete uštedjeti puno vremena s post-install konfiguracijom). (Na primjer F4 da izaberete vaš jezik kad ste pitani u boot-up stanju Live-CDa)

(U)EFI booting

The bootloader will be an EFI program installed to /boot/efi/EFI/aptosid/grubx64.efi within your EFI system partition and mounted below /boot/efi/ on your installed system, provided the following conditions are met:

  • The motherboard needs to have EFI firmware with EFI booting enabled.
  • A current aptosid-amd64.iso
  • Booted using UEFI and this is apparent from the plain white/blue grub2 menu, instead of the usual graphical boot menu provided by isolinux for BIOS booting, on the live medium.
  • You have a GPT disk with an EFI System Partition (type EF00). This must be vfat or FAT formatted.
  • The install target is not a USB disk. If you need to boot a USB stick see UEFI bootable removable devices.

UEFI bootable removable devices

In order to create a UEFI bootable device, such as a USB stick, the contents of the aptosid ISO can be extracted to a vfat formatted partition and the partition marked as bootable.

Optionally this removable medium can also be made compatible with PC-BIOS booting by installing a traditional boot sector in addition to the EFI boot loader (assuming /dev/sdz1 to be the target partition on the USB stick).

syslinux -i -d /boot/isolinux /dev/sdz1
install-mbr /dev/sdz

UEFI Secure Boot

aptosid does not currently support UEFI Secure Boot. The current intention is to wait to see how Debian will handle UEFI Secure Boot before deciding the best course of action for aptosid.

In order to allow dual/triple/quadruple booting of aptosid with other operating systems, UEFI Secure Boot needs to be disabled in the system firmware. The board firmware should offer a boot menu, although on some boards this may mean you need to boot into the MS OS Win 8 and cycle through various menus to turn off UEFI Secure Boot and then reboot.

Note: When you disable EFI Secure Boot and want to have MS Win8 as one of your Operating System choices you will need to reinstall MS Win8.

Rod Smith of rodsbooks.com provides a good insight on the subject of Secure Boot: Managing EFI Boot Loaders for Linux: Dealing with Secure Boot.

Choosing the language for your installation

Language Installs with KDE-full

Select your main language from the grub menu (F4) in the kde-full release, to install the localisations for the desktop and many applications while booting.

This ensures they are also present after installing aptosid, while only installing the required languages for the given system. The amount of memory required for this feature depends on the language and aptosid may refuse to install the given language packs automatically with insufficient RAM and the boot sequence will be continued in English language but with the desired locales settings (currency, date and time format, keyboard charsets). 1 GB memory or more should be safe for all supported languages, which are:

Default - Deutsch (German)
Default - English (English-US)
*Čeština (Czech)
*Dansk (Danish)
*Español (Spanish)
*English (GB)
*Français (French)
*Italiano (Italian)
*Nihongo (Japanese)
*Nederlands (Dutch)
*Polski (Polish)
*Português (Portuguese BR and PT)
*Română (Romanian)
*Русский (Russian)

The language selection depends on the availability of aptosid-manual translations, get involved to add your language.

Other Language installs with KDE-lite
  1. Select your main language from the gfxboot menu (F4). (See also aptosid specific Live-CD Cheatcodes). The Language files themselves are not on the Live-CD so the system will fall back to default English. However, this will make the correct language configuration needed for your preferred language and therefore no need to make any changes into the system, aside from the installation of the missing language files.
  2. Start the installation.
  3. Install to HD and reboot.
  4. After HD install, install the language of your choice and applications via apt-get.

Now choose where the installation is supposed to go to and we establish the mount points. Partitions for which we do not establish mount points for are mounted by the installer (the swap partition will be automatically mounted, when the system starts).

NOTE: If you have your root partition ('/") formatted with your preferred file system, you can uncheck the "format with" checkbox, provided that the drop box reflects the file system of your choice.

Sad se odlučimo gdje treba da bude instaliran i postavimo mount-points. Particije za koje ne postavite mount-point, instaler će postaviti. Ovdje možete izabrati root-particiju ("/") za vašu aptosid instalaciju. Sad isto možete kreirati particiju za podatke. A 'left-click' will activate your choices for each partition.

If you haven't partitioned your hard drive yet, click on the Execute button in the Start Part.-manager panel, also have a look at Partitioning your HD using Gparted or, if you want to use the shell, read Partitioning your HD

Također možete promjeniti vermensku zonu.

4. Dalje idemo s korisnikom, s lozinkom i root-lozinkom (dobro ih zapamtite!). Molimo vas ne odaberite jednostavne lozinke. To add additional users, do so after installation via the terminal with adduser..

5. Sad izaberite ime instalacije (možete je nazvati kako god hoćete, samo pazite da 'Hostname: hostname treba da se sastoji samo od slova (i brojki) i ne smije početi s brojem'.

After that you can choose whether ssh shall start automatically or not.

6. Ovo zadnje pitanje je vaša zadnja šansa da provjerite postavke koje ste napravili. Pročitajte ih dobro, onda kliknite na Spremi konfiguraciju i dalje..

U ovom stanju je moguče da promjenite/editirate config datoteku i da startujete instalaciju sa promjenenom konfiguracijom. Instaler ne provjerava više promjene zato ne smijete kliknuti na 'back' na instaleru inače će se izbrisati manualne promjene.

Editiranje (~/.sidconf) ja samo za iskustvene linux-korisnike, koje hoče da upišu svoje vlastite/specijalne promjene u config-datoteku ili im je potrebna specijalnal šema za particije na hard-disku, koju bih instaler odbaciju sa automatskim provjerama procesa.

Should the installer detect that you are trying to install over an old $HOME it will warn you and will not let you proceed with the installation until you choose another user name.

Da započnete instalaciju kliknite na Startuj Instalaciju Cijelome procesu treba, ovisno o vašem sistemu, oko 5 - 15 minuta, na starjim računalima može da traje do 60 minuta.

Ako napredna traka stoji nekoliko vremena, ne prekidajte, dajte procesu mal više vremena.

Gotovo! Izvadite CD. Sad restartujte u vašu novu HD instalaciju.

Prvi Bootup

Poslije prvog starta vidjeti će te da je aptosid zaboravio konfiguraciju umrežavanja. Tako da ga morate opet postaviti (WLAN, Modem, ISDN,...).

Ko god je postavio adrese umrežavanja automatski sa DHCP, koristeći DSL-router, mora ga reaktivirati s:

ceni

Opredijeljeni alati možete naći u Kmenu>aptosid>Internet>ceni. Isto odnosite se na: Internet i umrežavanje

To add an existing aptosid $home partition to new installation fstab needs to be altered, refer to Moving /home.

Do not use or share an existing $home from another distribution as the $home configuration files in a home directory will conflict if you share the same username between differing distributions.

To add users to your installation

To add a new user with automatic group permissions granted, as root: 

adduser <newuser>

Just press enter, it should take care of the complexities You will get asked to type in the password twice

aptosid specific icons (like the manual and IRC icons) need adding manually.

To delete a user

deluser <user>

Read

man adduser
man deluser

kuser can create new user as well, however you will need to manually adjust the group permissions for that user.

O sux

Razne naredbe potrebaju root privilegije. Da to možete obaviti upišite u konzoli:

sux

I ako je javna naredba za root privilegije 'su' koristeči sux umijesto toga će vam omogučiti da izvodite GUI / X11 aplikacije iz naredbene crte i dopušta root korisniku da startuje grafičke aplikacije, sux je omotač oko standardne su naredbe koji transferira vaše X vjerodajnice na namijenenog korisnika.

Some KDE applications require dbus-launch in front of the application:

dbus-launch <Application>

Primjer za izvođenje X11 aplikacije via sux je da koristite tekst editor kao kwrite ili kate editirate root datoteku, da particijonirate sa gparted ili Xapp menadžer za datoteke kao konqueror. Isto je moguče da promijenite root datoteke sa desnim klikom i izabiranjem 'edit-as-root', i upisanjem vaše root lozinke, to će pozvati kdesu za pozadinsko izvršavanje.

Unlike 'sudo', it means that someone can't just come along and type 'sudo' and make potentially damaging changes to your system.

UPOZORONJE: Dokle ste prijavljeni kao root, sistem vas neče zaustaviti kad izvršavate potencijalno opasne stvari kao brisanje važnih datoteka itd., morate biti apsolutno sigurni što radite, zato što je moguče da pokvarite cijeli sistem.

Po nikavim uvijetima ne izvodite aplikacije kao root u konzoli/terminalu koje standardni korisnik koristi svaki dan, da bude produktivan, kao salnje email, kreiranje tabela ili surfanje u internetu i tako dalje.
